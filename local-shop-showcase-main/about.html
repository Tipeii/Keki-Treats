Our Story

A legacy of Kenyan baking excellence

Baking Dreams Since 1985

Keki Treats began as a small family bakery in Nairobi's bustling CBD, founded by Mama Wanjiku with a simple dream: to share the authentic flavors of Kenyan baking with the world.

What started as a humble kitchen operation has grown into one of Nairobi's most beloved bakeries, known for our commitment to quality, tradition, and the warm hospitality that defines Kenyan culture.

Today, we continue Mama Wanjiku's legacy, using her original recipes and techniques while embracing modern innovations that enhance without compromising our heritage.
